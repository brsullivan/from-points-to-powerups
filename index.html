What is Gamification?

Gamification is the process of applying game mechanics to any non-game activity.

  • Gamification can be applied outside of tech solutions
  • Games come in many shapes & sizes
    • Video Games (console, mobile, multiplayer, singleplayer, online, casual)
    • Card Games (solitaire, poker)
    • Group party games (Mafia)
    • Brain Teasers (sudoku, crossword, SET)
    • Board Games (Clue, Sorry)
  • While you might not enjoy all of them, it's likely someone enjoys at least one of these

Game Mechanics

Game Mechanics can include:

  • Points
  • Achivements
  • Levels
  • Competition
  • Power-Ups
  • Challenges
